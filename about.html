Jack Desmond

cinematographer

Jack Desmond is a filmmaker from Cork. He studied at the National Film School, after making short films as a teenager.

 

He has directed, written and shot award winning short films.

 

His film "Speak To Her" has been screened across Europe. It won awards including 'Best Short Form (ROI)' and 'Best Writing' at the Royal Television Society Awards 2019 in London, which covered the best student films in the UK and Ireland.

 

He was awarded 'Best Cinematography' by the Royal Television Society awards in 2020.
