About

Jack Desmond is a director and cinematographer based in Cork and Belfast. He has worked with broadcasters such as RTÉ, TG4 and Al Jazeera.

He runs the Irish film and television production company Desmond Bros.

He is available for work in drama, factual or commercial work.

Jack works in the English and Irish languages.
