ABOUT

Jack Desmond is a filmmaker from Ireland. He studied at the National Film School, after making short films as a teenager.

 

He has directed, written and shot award winning short films.

 

His film "Speak To Her" has been screened across Europe, and has won awards including 'Best Short Form (ROI)' and 'Best Writing' at the Royal Television Society Awards.
