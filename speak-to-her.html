'Speak To Her'

'Speak To Her' (2018)

Directed by Jack Desmond

Produced by Philip Emo. Cinematography by Sean Doupe. Edited by Cian Desmond

Obafemi is an Eritrean bouncer in a Dublin club. He tries to work up the courage to speak to Natka, a bathroom attendant, learning Polish to overcome the language barrier between them.

Shot on Arri Alexa with Sony Cinealta lenses.

 

Winner 'Best Short Form' at the Royal Television Student Awards (ROI in Dublin)

 

Winner 'Best Writing' at the Royal Television Student Awards

(UK & ROI in London)

 

Shortlisted for Best Student Film at Fastnet Film Festival, 2019
